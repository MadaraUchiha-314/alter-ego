# Dissent log: <meeting title> — YYYY-MM-DD

Only real objections to a decision that was made anyway — not clarifying questions.
Makes "disagree and commit" explicit instead of passive-aggressive, and is valuable input
for later retros ("was the dissenter right?").

- **Decision overruled:** link to the `decisions.md` entry
  - **Objection:** what the dissenter argued, paraphrased or verbatim
  - **Raised by:** handle
  - **Reasoning to proceed anyway:** why the group went a different way
  - **Revisit:** whether/when to check if the dissenter was right (optional)
